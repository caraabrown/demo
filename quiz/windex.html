Step 4 — Resume Format
Choose your visual format.
Why do you need multiple resume versions? A single resume cannot win every room. A creative director reviewing your portfolio expects something different from an ATS scanner at a Fortune 500. Having format variants — same content, different visual architecture — means you can lead with the right signal for each context. The toolkit helps you maintain all versions and tailor each one per application.
Classic / Business
Clean hierarchy. ATS-safe. Best for corporate, consulting, finance, ops.
Two-Column / Tech
Sidebar skills column. Good for product, tech, and design ops roles.
Editorial / Creative
Bold header, expressive layout. Agencies, brand, design, content.
Performance / Metrics-Led
KPI callouts front-loaded. Operations, finance, and growth roles.
Sciences / Research
Dense, structured, publication-friendly. Research, healthcare, academia.
Minimal / Executive
White space. Restraint. VP, C-suite, and board-facing applications.
